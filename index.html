Why SiB

Three gaps. One platform.

SiB fills the missing link between Kanata North's best companies and the next generation of Ottawa talent — at zero cost to everyone.

🎓

Vetted Students

Every applicant completes a 5-step screening process. Resumes reviewed. Commitment verified. Only serious students reach employer profiles.

💼

Zero Cost to Employers

No fees, no subscriptions. Co-op placements are a genuine zero-cost way to bring motivated, school-board-supervised talent into your team.

✅

Co-op Credit Guaranteed

All placements run through Ottawa-area school co-op programs. School boards confirm credit — no extra paperwork for companies.

FAQ

Common questions

Is there a cost? ▼
No. This is a zero-cost program for everyone — students, employers, and schools. There are no fees of any kind.
Who can see a student's full details? ▼
Only the coordinator has full details at all times. Employers see first name + last initial, school, grade, skills, and availability. Full name and contact info are only shared after both parties confirm an intro meeting.
How are students screened? ▼
All students complete a 5-step application including a screening questionnaire, resume upload, and commitment agreement. The coordinator reviews each application manually before approving any profile.
Do employers need to be a KNBA member? ▼
No, but KNBA members receive expedited vetting. Non-members are welcome — the coordinator will verify your company is real, Kanata North-adjacent, and has a named supervisor.
How do I contact the coordinator? ▼
Email ysaadaldin08@gmail.com. Expected response time is 1–2 business days. All student-employer communication is routed through this address.
Can a student edit their application after submitting? ▼
Not directly. After submission, changes must be requested by emailing the coordinator. This protects the integrity of the review process.
